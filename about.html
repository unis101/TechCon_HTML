Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small local meetup for software engineers and innovators. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated technology conferences worldwide, drawing thousands of attendees and influential speakers from various sectors of the tech industry.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower the next generation of tech leaders by fostering innovation, sharing knowledge, and promoting inclusive discussions around the latest advancements in technology. We believe in building a community where ideas flourish and collaborations thrive.

Past Speakers

Ada Lovelace - Pioneer of computing theory. Her keynote emphasized the importance of ethics in AI.

Grace Hopper - Creator of COBOL, she spoke about legacy systems and the future of programming languages.

Tim Berners-Lee - Inventor of the World Wide Web. Shared insights on web decentralization and data ownership.
